# 🧠 Finter Neural Intelligence: AI Roadmap & Agentic Architecture

Finter is transitioning from a rule-based algorithm to a **Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL)** ecosystem. This document outlines the planned AI features and the integration of the **Finy.AI** neural engine.

---

## 🏛️ Phase 1: The Reinforcement Intelligence Layer (RIL)
*Goal: Enable the terminal to learn from every tick and every trade.*

### 1.1 Neural Memory Mapping (RL-Core)
- **Feature**: State-Action-Reward (SAR) Logging.
- **Logic**: Finter will map the "Market State" (Volatility, Spread, RSI, Trend, Session) to every trade outcome.
- **Output**: A "Neural Weight" file that the bot uses to filter out low-probability setups based on historical self-correction.

### 1.2 Multi-Agent Correlation Observer
- **Feature**: Autonomous "Correlator" Agents.
- **Logic**: Independent AI agents that monitor DXY (Dollar Index) and Gold concurrently. 
- **Action**: If the DXY agent detects an institutional sweep, it signals the EURUSD agent to freeze all SELL orders immediately.

---

## 🛰️ Phase 2: Finy.AI Agentic Interaction
*Goal: Transform Finy into a proactive trading co-pilot.*

### 2.1 Natural Language Execution (NLE)
- **Interface**: Chat-to-Terminal.
- **Capabilities**:
    - "Finy, move SL to Break-even for all Gold trades."
    - "Finy, what is the neural confidence for a BTC breakout right now?"
    - "Finy, stop trading until the NY Open volatility settles."

### 2.2 Global Sentiment Synthesis
- **Input**: Real-time LLM parsing of Bloomberg, Reuters, and DailyFX.
- **Logic**: Finy synthesizes thousands of headlines into a single **Sentiment Score (-1.0 to 1.0)**.
- **Impact**: Finter will only execute trades that align with the "Macro Narrative" synthesized by Finy.

---

## 🎯 Phase 3: Autonomous Strategy Evolution (Self-Healing)
*Goal: Zero-intervention trading.*

### 3.1 Adaptive Stop-Loss (Neural-Trailing)
- **Logic**: Instead of a fixed point trailing stop, the AI calculates the "Liquidity Void" below the current price.
- **Action**: The stop loss is moved to the exact point where the "Institutional Narrative" would be invalidated, not just a random distance.

### 3.2 Ghost-Shadow Trading (Pre-Alpha Validation)
- **Feature**: AI executes "Phantom Trades" in the background.
- **Logic**: Before a new strategy update goes live, the AI must prove profitability in a shadow environment for 24 hours.
- **Safety**: If the Shadow-Alpha drops below 60%, the live terminal is automatically locked.
